UniAdvisor App

Everything will become

easier with UniAdvisor App

UniAdvisor is a dedicated platform tailored for students navigating the intricate process of university selection in Turkey. At its core, the Preference Robot feature allows students to input their scores, subsequently generating a list of universities and courses they qualify for based on their preferences.

Beyond this crucial functionality, the application offers comprehensive information on universities, including address details, websites, and more. A standout feature is its capability to identify student dormitories near chosen universities and create transportation routes for easy commuting.

Moreover, those considering private institutions can delve into detailed insights comparing various private universities. This project encapsulates the culmination of rigorous computer engineering studies, designed to simplify and streamline the university selection process for countless students.
